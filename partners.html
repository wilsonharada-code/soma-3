The visitor is already here.

They have budget, time and intent. The question is whether they find you — or someone else.

OTAs and review platforms show your business to people still planning their trip — often months in advance, often to the wrong audience, always at a cost.

SomaVio works differently. We reach visitors who are already in Canmore or Kananaskis, with intent to explore today. Your offer appears when conditions and context make it relevant — not because you paid the highest bid.

A rainy afternoon triggers your indoor climbing wall. A morning hike completion triggers your café or spa. That's contextual visibility — and it's what SomaVio is built for.

How it works

Simple setup. No technical work on your end. Up and running in 48 hours.

01

Set up your profile

We build your profile together — name, category, hours, offers and the contexts where you want to appear. Takes about 30 minutes. We handle the rest.

02

Appear in the right moments

SomaVio matches your business to visitors based on weather, time of day, activity context and proximity. Your offer surfaces when it's genuinely relevant.

03

Track what's working

Partner Studio gives you visibility into profile views, offer clicks and which contexts are driving the most engagement — weekly, anonymized, no individual data.
