Indoor climbing · Rainy day · All levels · Family-friendly

What's good today

Rain moving in? Cold afternoon? The Climbing Lab is Canmore's best weather-proof activity for families and solo visitors. 10 to 50-foot walls, beginner to advanced. Staff on-site for instruction and safety. Day pass includes all gear — shoes, harness, chalk bag. No experience required — routes are colour-graded for all skill levels.
