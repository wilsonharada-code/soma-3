How it works for your hotel

SomaVio sits alongside your existing systems — it doesn't replace them. Three simple steps from setup to guest experience.

01

Set up your Guest Hub

We configure your hotel's SomaVio hub with your branding, hotel basics (WiFi, check-in, spa) and local context. Delivered via QR code in under 48 hours.

02

Guests scan and explore

No app download. Guests access SomaVio via QR code on check-in — and get real-time local guidance, safety updates and curated recommendations for their stay.

03

You see what's working

Through Partner Studio, you track engagement, manage offers and understand what your guests are exploring. Aggregated and anonymized — never individual data.

What your hotel gets

A complementary layer that adds value to your guest experience without replacing what already works.

Official safety always included

Trail conditions, wildlife alerts and weather updates from Parks Canada and Travel Alberta — automatically current, always attributed.

Contextual, not generic

Recommendations update based on real conditions — weather, trail status, time of day — so guests always get what's actually good right now.

No OTA commission

Fixed monthly fee. No commission on bookings. No percentage of revenue. A flat, predictable cost that fits your budget.

6 languages, zero effort

SomaVio auto-detects visitor language and serves content in EN, PT, ES, FR, JA and KO — no translation work required from your team.

No app. No friction.

Guests access everything via QR code — no download, no account, no password. Works on any smartphone, instantly.

Aggregated insights

Understand what your guests are exploring — by category, time of day and context. Anonymized and aggregated. Never individual data.
