Before you go

Check trail conditions before leaving — conditions change daily
Carry bear spray on all trails — and know how to use it
Tell someone your plan and expected return time
Check weather forecast — mountain weather changes fast
Download offline maps before heading out
Carry water and extra layers regardless of the forecast
Follow all posted signs and closures — they're there for good reason
Know the emergency number: 911 anywhere in Canada

In any emergency: call 911 immediately. For park emergencies, you can also reach Parks Canada Dispatch at 1-888-773-8888.
